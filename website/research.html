A Conceptual Model of Trust in Generative AI Systems

I had the privilege of collaborating with Dr. Rastegari and Dr. Tahmasbi as part of the research project, "A Conceptual Model of Trust in Generative AI Systems", which examines the factors that influence user trust in Generative Artificial Intelligence (AI). Our work emphasizes the critical role of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) research in developing ethical, user-centered technologies. The paper has been accepted for publication at the Hawaii International Conference on System Sciences (HICSS), highlighting the significance of our research. The project is still in progress of creating survey and collecting data. The final work will be updated as soon as the paper is officially published.
